Title: Fiona and Jane

Author: Jean Chen Ho

Rating:

Thoughts: I’m very rarely this negative when it comes to my reviews but this is a book that I don’t have many nice things to say. I also never give out 1 star reviews because there is usually always something that I can enjoy from a book, but this book didn’t deserve more than 1.

Firstly, I want to say this author has potential. There were so many moments in this book that I felt if the author had spent even a little bit more time thinking through her plot, developing her characters, and sticking to what the story was actually supposed to be about (friendship) there would have been something good here.

There’s no structure to this book. The entire time random events are happening to our main characters and we never quite understanding their relevance, or care. This book is supposed to be all about Fiona and Janes friendship throughout their lives together, but the whole time I couldn’t help thinking to myself “why are these two even friends?

This is the risk picking up a book you know nothing about. But I’ll keep doing it, because the book I’m reading right now that I knew nothing about is looking like a 5 star read.

Just don’t read it. And if you do, let me know if you disagree, because I hope someone found joy from this book.
