Title: River Sing Me Home

Author: Eleanor Shearer

Rating:

Thoughts: I was pretty excited to read River Sing Me Home. The synopsis sounded powerful, a women exscapes from enslavement to find her long lost children and we get to embark on the journey with her. I really like the premise of this book but the execution did fall short for me.

I felt like everything was rushed. It seemed like everything happened a little too easily for our protagonist, which doesn't make sense given the premise of the good. I didn't feel like I had time to worry or care about the characters, because everything happened so quickly.

Overall, I would say read this book is you want to learn something new and if the synopsis interests you. But don't expect something really detailed that you can dive into for a long period of time.
