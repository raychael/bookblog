Title: Daisy Darker

Author: Alice Feeney

Rating:

Thoughts: I have some pretty fond memories thinking back to reading Daisy Darker. This book kept my attention and has me curious to continue reading, never growing bored of the plot. I also loved the links to And Then There Were None by Agatha Christie that were left throughout this book. I read that book for the first time not that long ago and would honestly recommnd reading that one first if you haven't yet, just so you get the references.

Daisy Darker is a murder mystery novel that is done in a very fun and creative way. There's also the aspect of the rich, toxic family where everyone's a bit of a suspect, which is always the best.

My only complaint was the ending was a bit anticlimatic and could have been a lot more realistic and spooky had the author gone in a different direction. But overall I still have a lot of fond memories thinking back to reading Daisy Darker.
