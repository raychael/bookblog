Title: After

Author: Anna Todd

Rating:

Thoughts: When I was in early high school I vividly remember reading all of Anna Todds books on Wattpad, and I remember absolutely devouring them. I've also watched all the movies (they're a total guilty pleasure for me) so I wanted to revisit Todd's series and see how I felt after all these years.

I must have been naive, because I assumed I would still loved it. I was super excited to meet up with Harry, oh I mean "Hardin" and Tessa and basically just get a refresher. Unfortunately, that's not what happened. I got tired of the repetitive party seems, the fighting, the toxic friend group, the cheating, etc. very quickly. I actually became painful at the end to force myself to finish this book and I found myself reaching for anything else to read.

Overall, I now understand why so many people don't like this book.
