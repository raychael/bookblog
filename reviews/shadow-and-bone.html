Title: Shadow and Bone

Author: Leigh Bardugo

Rating:

Thoughts: This was an interesting one to read but sitting here thinking back on this book it wasn't very memorable. I like this idea of this series, I also really like the magical world, my main problem was the cast wasn't very memorable and I wasn't emotionally invested in any of the outcomes.

However, I did really like the concept of this book. I loved the Grisha world and all the magic happening within it. I also think the Darkling is a really interesting character. I would have loved more on him throughout this book actually.

I pretty much despise our love interest though and am really hoping throughout the rest of the series there's someone better. (The Darkling??)
