Title: A Court of Wings and Ruin

Author: Sarah J. Maas

Rating:

Thoughts: My interested in the ACOTAR series is pretty much gone. It makes me sad because I did genuinly really like the first book of the series, A Court of Thorns and Roses. I think there's just something about a cast that's so wildly perfect, beautiful, and powerful that comes off very uninteresting to me. And I wish authors would stop doing it.

I actually did enjoy this book more than A Court of Mist and Fury. It kept my attention longer and I thought bringing back Lucien into the series and having him play a role in Feyre's new life was interesting. However, I'm not sure if I can take much more of Feyre's sisters, which is why I'm feeling unmotivated to continue.

Overall, I'm glad so many people find a lot of joy from this series. I'm just not one of those people.
