Title: Dating Dr. Dil

Author: Nisha Sharma

Rating:

Thoughts: It wasn’t that I thought there was anything particularly bad about this book. I really liked the emphasis on Indian culture that Sharma brought to the story. The descriptions of Indian dishes, outfits, and family dynamics was really interesting. But the thing I didn’t particularly like about this book was that there really wasn’t anything real or special about our main characters other than how hot and perfect they both were, which just isn’t going to keep my attention for 300+ pages.

This entire book seems to center around a couple that can’t seem to end up together for no actual legitimate reason other than they are both stubborn. And we move slowly along with our couple fighting not to be together and insisting there is no love involved when there clearly is.

I think if you are looking for a love story that doesn’t go very far past surface level issues you might enjoy this, it just wasn’t what I was looking for.
