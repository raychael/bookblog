Title: Carrie Soto is Back

Author: Taylor Jenkins Reid

Rating:

Thoughts: I have a confession to make, I was a little scared to read Carrie Soto is back for a couple of reasons. The first being Malibu Rising was my favorite book read in 2021 (and one of my all time favorites), so there was a lot of expectations for another TJR book. The second was Carrie wasn't exactly the most likable character in Malibu Rising, and I was worried about how I would feel reading an entire book about her. However, as you can see by the 5 star rating up above, I has some very unfound worries! So unfound as a matter of fact Carrie Soto was my favorite book OF THE YEAR!

Carrie Soto is back is about a retired tennis player making a comeback to defend her title. It's about a complicated but loving father-daughter realtionships, determination, pride, romance, and everything in between. This was the only book I've read in a long time that I phsyically could not bring myself to stop reading. I can't recommend this book enough.
