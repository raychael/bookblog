Title: Killers of a Certain Age

Author: Deanna Raybourn

Rating:

Thoughts: I was really excited about the premise of this book when I first heard about it. I was even more excited because it was October so I couldn't think of a better time to read this type of cozy murder read.

There were aspects of this book I really liked. I really liked the team of all female assassins. I liked how different the characters were and the the flashbacks to when these women first got recruited. My main issue was that I was a lot more interested in the flashbacks then I was to our present day characters. I thought actually seeing our group in training and the initial dynamics of the group were much more exciting. I know the whole point of the book is that the women are older, but I can't help how I feel.

Overall, I didn't love it or hate it. And I haven't thought much about it since reading.
