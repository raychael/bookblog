Title: Siege and Storm

Author: Leigh Bardugo

Rating:

Thoughts: Siege and Storm was definitely a less enjoyable reading experience for me as compared to Shadow and Bone. Maybe it was how much Mal was involved in this book (I hate him) or how all the aspects I liked about Alina in the first book have been erased and she is now the basic all powerful magical main character that's seen everywhere. Maybe it's just me but this trope get's old.

Another issue is that I didn't follow what was happening in this book very well. This doesn't happen to me a lot, only with books I'm having trouble paying attention to. So I'm not even sure how to summerize it.

There were parts that I still enjoyed. The scenes with the Darkling were fun, and the battle scenes were nice. I also enjoyed the moments with characters coming back from book one and seeing where they were at. But all in all this series has been a no for me.
