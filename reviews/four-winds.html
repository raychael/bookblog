Title: The Four Winds

Author: Kristin Hannah

Rating:

Thoughts: Kristin Hannah is still going strong being one of my favorite authors of all time. Every book I read by her is so captivating while also being educational and serious, I love it.

The Four Winds is a historical fiction revolving around the Great Depression and the Dust Bowl and its impact on a farming family. This book got a lot of emotions out of me, a lot of which were not exactly positive, but in the best way that the lovely Kirstin Hannah does.

This book has a lot to do with relationship dynamics, which I always love. Between mothers and daughters, spouses, siblings, friends, and neighbors. It also has to do with survival and sacrifices and forgiveness. Everything Hannah is so amazing at.

Yes, this book did lose a star from me, but thatâ€™s only because it was a slow book and I wanted it to be a little more speedy. Overall though, I still say to read it.
