Title: And Then There Were None

Author: Andy Agatha Christie

Rating:

Thoughts: I'm a little embarrassed to admit that this is the first Agatha Christie book I've ever read. But I'm less embarrassed to admit how much I enjoyed it and how much I'm looking forward to reading more of her books. (I just picked up Murder of the Orient Express).

I don't think I have to say too much about The Agatha Christies most famous book. It's suspensful, clever, and creepy. And I loved the murdery poem idea.

I also love that even though I didn't read this book all that long ago, I see references everywhere. I'll see references on TV shows, other books, everywhere. So, if you somehow haven't read this book yet like I hadn't, you need to. One piece of advice though, spent some time keeping track of all the characters, it gets confusing.
