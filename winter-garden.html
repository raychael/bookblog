Title: Winter Garden

Author: Kristin Hannah

Rating:

Thoughts: I said it once and I’ll say it again, I love Kristin Hannah. I love the relationships with the characters she manages to build, I love the scenes, I love the deeper messages, and I love the writing.

I will say, however, that Winter Garden was not my favorite Kristin Hannah book. In fact, it wasn’t even my second or third favorite, but it was good. What I struggled with the most in this book was how much I wanted to read about World War II Russia, and how much I instead read about the present day from the view of the daughters who never experienced the war themselves and the fairytale version their mother chose to tell them instead. I would have preferred more time inside Anya’s head and less time on fairytales, but it was an interesting way to tell the story nonetheless.
