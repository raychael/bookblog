Title: Ramón and Julieta

Author: Alana Quintana Albertson

Rating:

Thoughts: I loved this book for a couple of significant reasons. The first one being I was actually in Mexico at the time of reading the book, so the setting was absolutely perfect and definitely added to my enjoyment. The second being this is simply an extremely enjoyable and fun romance read if you're in that cute romantic reading mood.

Ramón and Julieta is a Romeo and Juliet retelling (if you didn't already guess that from the name). Despite the Romeo and Juliet connection though you don't have to worry about it being super insta-lovey, in fact in turns more into an enemies to lovers trope than anything else.

I would really recommend this book overall, especially if you plan to head over to Mexico anytime soon. (Or even just San Diego, as that is where the book is actually based).
