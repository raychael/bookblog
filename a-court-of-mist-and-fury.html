Title: A Court of Mist and Fury

Author: Sarah J. Maas

Rating:

Thoughts: Lately, I've been having some bad luck with series not turning into to what I'm hoping for, and not turning into something I want to finish.

Something that really intrigued me about the first book were the characters, such as Tamlin, Lucien, Amarantha etc. I also really appreciated having a more relatable, down to earth main character. And who can forget the amazing trials Feyre went through at the end of the book that led me so badly wanting to get my hands on the second.

A Court of Mist and Fury goes in the opposite direction of what I liked so much about the first book. We don't even see the majority of the characters from the first book the majority of the time. We also have now moved from our relatable main character to a much more clich√© one that's all powerful and going to save everyone, not to mention the all powerful group she's conventiently found herself into.
