Title: Howl's Moving Castle

Author: Diana Wynne Jones

Rating:

Thoughts: The thing about this book is I don’t really think I can rate it fairly. I watched the movie not long before reading the book and because of the similarities it was hard for me to stay entertained. I felt in a lot of ways like I was sitting and rewatching the movie again, just with less fun visuals and it was taking a lot longer.

My advice with this one is to either read the book before you watch the movie or wait long enough between watching to movie and reading the book that you forget a lot of the plot, because it really is very similar in a lot of ways.

Overall, I would try another book by Diana Wynne Jones, I just won’t watch the movie first.
