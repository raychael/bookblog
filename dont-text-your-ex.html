Title: Don't Text Your Ex Happy Birthday

Author: Nick Viall

Rating:

Thoughts: I got this book as a Christmas present from my boyfriend because I'm a big Viall Files (the podcast) fan. I even started watching The Bachelor just to listen to their recaps on the podcast. With that being said I think this is a really good book for the appropriate audience and that was not me.

My expectation was that this book was going to be more well rounded in terms of speaking to both single people and those in relationships, since that's how his podcast is. Unfortunately, 90% of the book only applied to those currently in the dating market. Pretty ironic since my boyfriend was the one who gifted it to me! However, I think this is a really fun and helpful book for those looking to get in to, or are cuurently in, the dating world. You just have to be ready to hear some advice that might not be the most pleasant.

This book is also written in a very casual way, which might annoy some readers. Other than that if the topic applies give this book a chance.
