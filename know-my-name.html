Title: Know My Name

Author: Chanel Miller

Rating:

Thoughts: (TW) I think this is one of the most powerful, well written, and educational memories I have ever read. Chanel Miller is an amazing writer, a strong individual, and overall deserves all the praise for this book. She is most widely known for being the anonymous girl in the Brock Turner sexual assault case at Stanford.

Miller goes through the entire process with her readers from where everything started to now, years later still recovering and helping others. She talks about the court process, which was very interesting to hear from a victims perfective. She talks about all the press, keeping this secret from her family and friends, and what the trauma did to her mentally.

This is a difficult book to read, It will make you angry and sad and probably bring tears to your eyes. It will also open your eyes a little bit more to the reality of sexual assault cases and what it does to it's victims. I highly recommend.
