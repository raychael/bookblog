Title: Being Mortal

Author: Atul Gawande

Rating:

Thoughts: This is the second book by Atul Gawande I've read and the second book by him I've been completely engrossed by. Being mortal is a complex book, but to put it simply it's about getting old and experiencing those around us getting old and dealing with death. This book was not just an entertaining book. It was valuable and informative and made me think a lot about how we treat the elderly. My plan is to eventually pick this book up when it becomes more relevant to my life and actually utilize the advice given.

This book is going to be particularly powerful for those who work in health care or those who have a relative who is older or getting close to the end of their life. However. I don't fall into either of these categories and I still very much valued my time reading.

5/5 I recommend.
