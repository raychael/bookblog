Title: Beach Read

Author: Emily Henry

Rating:

Thoughts: Fun fact, I was nervous going into this book because I wasn’t the biggest fan of People We Meet On Vacation (Emily Henry’s other very popular romance novel). What made me pick it up was actually Haley Pham, a popular booktuber, saying she loved Beach Read despite also not loving People We Meet on Vacation.

I hadn’t read a romance book in a while when I picked up this one and now I’ve been launched right back into the romance genre from how much I loved this book. From the cute dates to the vulnerability of our main character to the talk of books and writing. I loved it.

If you’re wondering why this book was 4 stars and not 5 there are a significant amount of pages in the middle where the story is slow and I became uninterested, but it picked up again.

I loved reading about January’s sensitive side when it came to Gus. I do know this is a reason why

some readers didn’t like January, but I found it realistic and relatable. It’s not always fun to read about a character was has it all together.

Gus was one of the more likable men I’ve read about in an enemies to lovers book. And that’s probably because this wasn’t really a true enemies to lovers. He’s sweet and had a lot of chemistry with our main character.

Overall, I say read it. I don’t think you’ll regret it.
