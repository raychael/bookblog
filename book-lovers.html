Title: Book Lovers

Author: Emily Henry

Rating:

Thoughts: I think it’s pretty much impossible for a new book to compete with my love for Emily Henry’s “Beach Read” but I was hoping this one would. There was a lot I liked about this book. I loved our main character, Nora. A smart, career driven, sassy woman from NYC who is totally true to herself. What made me sad about this book is I really wanted to see Nora in her element. Going around New York, exploring new places, seeing shows, and being in the bustle of the town. Instead, we got her in a small town the entire book, completely out of her element.

Another issue I had with this book was there wasn’t very much going on. I was never in a moment where I was dying to know what happened next or staying up late to finish another chapter, I just didn’t care that much.

However, I’ve heard glowing reviews about this book, so it’s worth the shot if you are an Emily Henry fan. It just wasn’t for me.
