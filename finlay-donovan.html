Title: Finlay Donovan is Killing It

Author: Elle Cosimano

Rating:

Thoughts: I absolutely loved this book and had such a genuinely good time reading it. Finlay Donovan is a cozy murder mystery, meaning you don’t have to worry about reading this late at night and not being able to sleep. It’s silly, entertaining, witty, and all and all a good time. It actually reminded me of another cozy murder mystery I read in 2021 called Arsenic and Adobo, (another one I highly recommend).

This isn’t by any means a book you will read and think it’s realistic, complex, or anything like that. It’s all for fun. So if you don’t like cheesy humor in your books I would probably stay away from this one.

But if you aren’t like that and you just want to read a book that’s going to put you in a good mood and make you forget about your worries, I say pick this up. Also there’s a sequel called Finlay Donovan Knock ‘Em Dead that I am super excited to pick up, I just need to wait for the paperback version
