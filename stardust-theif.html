Title: The Stardust Theif

Author: Chelsea Abdullah

Rating:

Thoughts: This book actually disappointed me quite a bit in the long run. When I first started reading it had all the potential to be a 5 star read. It had a super interesting arabian setting, genies, princes, theives, and magic. However. as time went on the book fell pretty short for me and I found myself waiting for it to end.

It seemed to me that more than anything the author didn't know where to go with this story, or they just went in a direction that made no sense to me.

The story is going to continue into a series, but I can't see myself continuing. Especially because of my lack of connection to any of the characters.
