Title: Harry Potter and the Half Blood Prince

Author: J.K. Rowling

Rating:

Thoughts: Personally, I don’t think any of the Harry Potter books really need ratings at this point. I’m sure you can do a quick Google search and find Harry Potter reviews by the truck full.

However, I’ve never read the Harry Potter series before, so all of these emotions are very new and I feel I need to share.

I loved this book. Loved it so much in fact I think this is my new favorite book in the entire series. (This may change after Deathly Hallows, we shall see). I could not get over the drama that happened with Snape in this book and the character development we got to see among our cast of characters, like Ginny.

Overall I’m loving this series and have already started the last book. I hope it’s as good as this one!
