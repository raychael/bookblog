Title: The House in the Cerulean Sea

Author: TJ Klune

Rating:

Thoughts: I cannot express how absolutely in love with this book I am. I found myself smiling and actually laughing out loud while reading, which is something I don’t do often. Klune has a great sense of humor. It’s the perfect amount of dry and self deprecating while also coming off completely natural. I loved it.

I also loved out characters. I couldn’t help growing attached to the magical children in this book the same way our main character does. They are all so unique and different but relatable to regular children despite their powers.

There’s also a romance that is pretty exciting to follow. Read this! I know I’ll be reading “Under the Whispering Door” (Klune’s newest book) ASAP.
