Title: Home Before Dark

Author: Riley Sager

Rating:

Thoughts: Home Before Dark is the first Riley Sager book I’ve ever read and while I’m not super eager to pick up another one of his books I’m glad I got a feel for his story telling and writing skills since I keep hearing so much about him.

There were positive and negatives to this book. One negative I want to say that might not be a negative for everyone is I wasn’t at all scared reading this book. Maybe Sager’s point isn’t to scare you, but I wanted to be at least a little bit spooked reading this and I didn’t so much as think about anything scary after reading or get hung up on any screen. In fact there was one scene (snakes) that I found myself cringing at more than anything.

What I will say though is that the story was entertaining, the characters were relatable, and if you want to read a haunted house book but are not looking to be scared, this book might just be perfect for you.
