Title: A Court of Thorns and Roses

Author: Sarah J. Maas

Rating:

Thoughts: No words can describe how excited I am to continue reading the rest of this series. (Iâ€™ve already start A Court of Mist and Fury.) And how excited I am go see what happens to our main character after that amazing ending.

A Court of Thorns and Roses starts out super interesting. We follow our main character, a regular human girl, into a mysterious magical land with fairies. The book starts off really exciting but I will say it does dull in the middle. We have a large portion of the book where it feels like nothing much is happening and all we are able to focus on is the blossoming friendship/more between our two main characters.

The really good part of this book comes later, when we get into the more sinister part of the book and get to see what our main character is capable of, and get to know from other characters a little better too. I recommend.
